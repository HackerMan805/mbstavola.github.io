About

My name is Matthew Stavola (or Matthew Freitas, depending which parent you ask) and I'm a programmer by trade.

I graduated from St. John's University in 2015 with a BS in Computer Science. For the 2014-2015 term I was also the president of the Computer Science Society, although my term was cut short because I graduated early.

I really love learning new things and messing around with them, which is why hackathons are so important to me! Everyone should have the experience of working on a cool project with their friends through the weekend.

Currently, I am employed by LIFARS as the Lead Software Engineer. I get to work with F/OSS and in a bunch of different languages! We also are developing an Android app and some other cool stuff.

During my free time I'm usually either playing games or making them, but I also enjoy reading manga and drinking great beer.
